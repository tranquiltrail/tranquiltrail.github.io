About Kolad

Kolad, nestled in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India, is a quaint village that beckons nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Surrounded by the lush Sahyadri mountain range, Kolad is renowned for its scenic beauty and exhilarating outdoor activities. The Kundalika River, originating from Tamhini Ghat, flows through this region, making it a hotspot for white-water rafting aficionados.

Beyond rafting, Kolad offers a spectrum of adventures including trekking, rappelling, and camping. Its verdant landscapes and tranquil ambience provide an ideal backdrop for nature lovers to immerse themselves in the serene surroundings.

The village also boasts a historical heritage with sites like Tala Fort and Ghosala Fort, offering a glimpse into the area's rich past. Kolad's convenient location, nestled between Mumbai and Pune, makes it a favored weekend retreat for urban dwellers.

With its harmonious blend of natural splendor, adrenaline-pumping activities, and historical charm, Kolad stands as an inviting destination for those seeking a refreshing escape from the rigors of urban life.

Things To Do

Kolad, Maharashtra, offers a plethora of activities for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Here are some things to do in Kolad:

1. White Water Rafting:

Experience the thrill of rafting on the Kundalika River, known for its challenging rapids and stunning scenery.

2. Trekking:

Explore the picturesque Sahyadri mountains by embarking on one of the many scenic trekking trails in and around Kolad.

3. Kayaking:

Navigate the calm waters of the Kundalika River on a kayak, enjoying a more leisurely water adventure.

4. Zip-lining and Rappelling:

Get an adrenaline rush by zip-lining across valleys or rappelling down steep slopes, taking in breathtaking views along the way.

5. Camping:

Set up a tent amidst the natural beauty of Kolad for a memorable overnight experience. Enjoy bonfires, stargazing, and the sounds of nature.

6. Visit Tamhini Ghat:

Explore the scenic Tamhini Ghat, known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

7. Visit Kuda Caves:

Discover the ancient Kuda Caves, a complex of rock-cut Buddhist caves, which offer a glimpse into the region's rich history.

8. Bird Watching:

Kolad and its surrounding areas are home to a variety of bird species. Bring your binoculars and enjoy birdwatching in this natural haven.

9. Visit Ghosala Fort:

Explore the historic Ghosala Fort, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and the Kundalika River.

10. Explore Local Villages:

Take a cultural detour by visiting nearby villages, interacting with locals, and learning about their way of life.

Remember to follow safety guidelines and respect the environment while participating in these activities. Enjoy your time in the adventurous and scenic surroundings of Kolad!

How To Reach

Kolad, a picturesque village in Maharashtra, India, is accessible by various modes of transportation. Here are the ways to reach Kolad:

By Air:

- The nearest major airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, which is approximately 120 kilometers away from Kolad. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach Kolad.

By Train:

- The nearest railway station is Kolad Railway Station itself, which is well-connected to major cities like Mumbai and Pune. Several trains run on the Mumbai-Goa route and stop at Kolad. You can also reach Kolad by taking a train to Roha Railway Station, which is approximately 30 kilometers away, and then taking a local taxi or bus to Kolad.

By Road:

- Kolad is well-connected by road to major cities in Maharashtra. You can either hire a private taxi, take a bus, or drive your own vehicle. The roads leading to Kolad offer scenic views of the surrounding Sahyadri mountains.

Travel Tips:

- It's advisable to check the schedules of trains and buses in advance, especially during weekends and holidays. - Confirm the availability of transportation options from your point of origin to Kolad, and consider making advance bookings if necessary. - Always have a map or GPS navigation system handy, especially if you're driving, to ensure you stay on the correct route.

Remember to plan your journey according to your preferences, budget, and convenience. Enjoy your trip to the adventurous and scenic destination of Kolad!
