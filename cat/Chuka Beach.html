About Chuka Beach

Unlike the many beaches in Goa, this beach isn't for soaking up the sun in your beachwear. While we would not mind spending hours by the beachside in Goa, the good thing for those hard-pressed for time and money is that there is a beach in the state of Uttar Pradesh still not known to many. Luckily, not everyone needs to rush to an exotic destination to have a tropical escape. Chuka Beach, close to the Himalayan forests in Uttar Pradesh (UP), has plenty to offer for a beach getaway.

Not popular in the tourist circuit in North India, Chuka Beach has a strong potential to grow as a tourist spot. It is actually a water reservoir of Sharda river barrage. It falls under the Mahof forest range, between Sharda Sagar Dam and Sharda Canal, in Pilibhit district. The area surrounding Chuka Beach is protected by the state government, and has been converted into a picnic spot so as to promote tourism. The region enclosing Chuka Beach is marked for the religious shrines and pervading peace and serenity. A 2 km ride through the dense forest leads to the Chuka Beach. Apparently, this place is famous for its big cats, fox, jackals and so on. A canteen, run by the government is where you can get tea and snacks for the visitors.

Things To Do

Chuka Beach is a relatively untouched natural beauty and attracts locals and tourists seeking solitude and relaxation. The beach has clean sand, clear water and lush greenery.

1.Relax on the Beach -

Chuka Beach is beautiful and peaceful, ideal for those seeking a relaxing day out. Visitors can sunbathe on the sandy shore, stroll along the beach, or sit back and enjoy the stunning views.

2.Swimming -

The waters at Chuka Beach are calm and clear, making it an ideal spot for swimming. Visitors can take a refreshing dip in the cool waters and enjoy a fun day with family and friends.Picnic -Chuka Beach is a popular spot for picnics and day trips. Visitors can bring food and enjoy a fun-filled day amidst stunning natural beauty.

3.Boating -

Visitors can enjoy boating on the Sharda River, which runs alongside Chuka Beach. Boating is a fun activity that allows visitors to explore the river and its surroundings.

4.Photography -

Chuka Beach is a picturesque destination, offering visitors stunning views of the surrounding landscape.Photography enthusiasts can capture the beauty of the beach and its surroundings through their lenses.

5.Bird Watching -

Chuka Beach is home to several species of birds, making it an ideal destination for birdwatchers. Visitors can spot several species of birds, including kingfishers, cormorants, and egrets

How To Reach

Chuka is a village located in the Pilibhit district of Uttar Pradesh, India. To reach Chuka in Pilibhit, you can follow these general guidelines:

By Road:

- Chuka is accessible by road. You can drive to Chuka from various nearby cities and towns in Uttar Pradesh and other parts of India. The specific route you should take will depend on your starting location. It's recommended to use a navigation app or GPS to find the best route.

By Train:

- The nearest railway station to Chuka is Pilibhit Junction. After reaching Pilibhit Junction, you can hire a taxi or take a local bus to reach Chuka, which is approximately 20-30 kilometers away. Ensure you check the train schedule and availability before planning your trip.

By Air:

- The nearest airport with regular commercial flights is Pantnagar Airport in Uttarakhand, which is located at a distance of around 80-90 kilometers from Pilibhit. After arriving at Pantnagar Airport, you can proceed to Chuka by hiring a taxi or taking a bus.

Chuka is a small village, so transportation options and connectivity may be limited. Make sure to check the latest transportation options and accommodations in advance if you plan to visit Chuka, especially if you're traveling from a distant location. It's also advisable to use a reliable navigation tool or map for directions.
