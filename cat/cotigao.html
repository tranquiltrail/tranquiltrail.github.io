About Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary, situated in the southern part of Goa, is a hidden gem known for its rich biodiversity and lush, pristine landscapes. Covering an area of approximately 86 square kilometers, this sanctuary provides a tranquil escape into nature. It's one of the state's oldest protected areas and offers a unique opportunity to witness Goa's natural beauty and wildlife.

The sanctuary's dense forests are home to a variety of flora and fauna, including diverse species of birds, butterflies, and mammals. Among its inhabitants, you may spot leopards, sloth bears, gaurs, and various species of deer. The highlight of Cotigao is the towering treetop watchtower that allows visitors to observe the surrounding wildlife in their natural habitat.

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary is an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts and those seeking an off-the-beaten-path experience in Goa. It's a place to connect with the tranquility of the wild and discover the hidden treasures of the state's southern region.

Things To Do

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary in Goa offers a serene and immersive experience in the heart of nature. While it may not be a destination for a wide range of activities, there are several things you can do when visiting the sanctuary:

1. Wildlife Watching: Explore the sanctuary's trails and keep an eye out for various wildlife species, including deer, gaurs, langurs, and if you're lucky, leopards and sloth bears. The treetop watchtower provides an excellent vantage point for wildlife observation.

2. Birdwatching: Cotigao is home to numerous bird species. Birdwatchers can bring their binoculars and spend time identifying and observing the diverse avian inhabitants.

3. Nature Walks: Take leisurely walks through the sanctuary's lush forests. Enjoy the tranquility of the natural surroundings and appreciate the diverse flora and fauna.

4. Photography: Capture the beauty of the sanctuary's landscapes, wildlife, and plant life through photography. The scenic vistas and the peaceful ambiance provide excellent opportunities for photography.

5. Visit the Interpretation Center: Learn more about the sanctuary's ecology and inhabitants at the interpretation center, which offers educational exhibits and information about the local flora and fauna.

6. Picnicking: Pack a picnic and enjoy a meal amidst the serene surroundings of the sanctuary. There are designated picnic areas where you can relax and unwind.

7. Stay in Forest Cottages: Cotigao offers forest cottages for overnight stays, allowing you to experience the tranquility of the sanctuary during the early morning and evening hours.

8. Respect the Environment: While exploring the sanctuary, it's essential to follow eco-friendly practices, such as not littering and minimizing noise to respect the wildlife.

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary is a peaceful retreat where you can immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Goa. It's a destination for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and those seeking a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

How To Reach

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary in Goa is located in the southern part of the state and can be reached via road. Here's how to get to Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary:

By Air:

- The nearest airport is Goa International Airport (Dabolim Airport), which is well-connected to major Indian cities and international destinations. After arriving at the airport, you can hire a taxi or rent a car to reach Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary. The sanctuary is approximately 60-70 kilometers from the airport.

By Train:

- The nearest railway station to Cotigao is Canacona Railway Station, which is well-connected to major Indian cities. After arriving at Canacona Railway Station, you can hire a taxi or take a local bus to reach the sanctuary. The distance between Canacona and Cotigao is approximately 15-20 kilometers.

By Road:

- You can also reach Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary by road from various parts of Goa. The sanctuary is located near the town of Canacona in South Goa. You can hire a taxi or drive yourself to the sanctuary, following local signposts or using GPS navigation. The sanctuary is well-marked, making it relatively easy to find.

Once you arrive in the vicinity of Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary, you can inquire about specific directions to the sanctuary from locals or at nearby information centers. The sanctuary is a serene destination, offering a peaceful escape into nature for those looking to explore Goa's natural beauty and wildlife.
