About Shivanasamudra Falls

Shivanasamudra Falls, located in Karnataka, is a mesmerizing natural wonder that showcases the raw, untamed beauty of water and rock. These falls, formed by the River Kaveri, are divided into two segments: Gaganachukki and Bharachukki. The sheer force and thundering sound of the water as it descends from the rocky cliffs create a captivating spectacle.

Gaganachukki Falls, known for its horsetail shape, offers a panoramic view from the watchtower. Bharachukki Falls, on the other hand, boasts a wider cascade and is surrounded by lush greenery, offering a tranquil and picturesque setting.

The area around Shivanasamudra is rich in flora and fauna, making it an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy activities like trekking, birdwatching, and capturing the scenic beauty through photography. Shivanasamudra Falls is not only a visual delight but also a testament to the awe-inspiring power of nature.

Things To Do

Shivanasamudra Falls in Karnataka is a stunning natural attraction with plenty of activities to enjoy. Here are some things to do when visiting Shivanasamudra Falls:

1. Scenic Viewing: Marvel at the breathtaking views of the falls. You can observe Gaganachukki Falls and Bharachukki Falls from designated viewpoints. The sight and sound of the cascading water are awe-inspiring.

2. Photography: Capture the beauty of the falls and the surrounding landscapes. The falls offer excellent photography opportunities, so don't forget your camera.

3. Nature Walks: Take a leisurely walk around the area to immerse yourself in the lush greenery and appreciate the natural beauty.

4. Birdwatching: The surrounding forests are home to various bird species. Bring your binoculars and try to spot the diverse avian life.

5. Trekking: There are trekking opportunities in the nearby hills and forests. Enquire locally about guided treks that may be available.

6. Visit Talakadu: Shivanasamudra is close to the historic town of Talakadu. Explore its temples, sand dunes, and the cultural heritage of the region.

7. River Rafting: The downstream of the Kaveri River offers river rafting opportunities. It's an exhilarating experience for adventure seekers.

8. Picnic: Pack a picnic and enjoy a meal in the serene surroundings of Shivanasamudra Falls.

9. Religious Sites: Explore the nearby temples, including the Ranganatha Swamy Temple and the Arkeshwara Temple, which are culturally significant.

10. Boat Rides: Depending on the water levels, boat rides may be available to take you closer to the falls.

Shivanasamudra Falls is a place where you can relax in the lap of nature, appreciate its beauty, and engage in various outdoor activities. Whether you're a nature lover, an adventurer, or a history enthusiast, there's something for everyone to enjoy at this picturesque destination.

How To Reach

Shivanasamudra Falls, located in Karnataka, is accessible by road and can be reached from various cities and towns in the region. Here's how to reach Shivanasamudra Falls:

By Air:

- The nearest airport to Shivanasamudra Falls is the Kempegowda International Airport in Bangalore, which is approximately 140-150 kilometers away. After arriving at the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the falls. The journey by road usually takes around 3-4 hours.

By Train:

- The nearest railway station to Shivanasamudra Falls is Maddur Railway Station, which is well-connected to cities like Bangalore, Mysore, and other major towns in Karnataka. After reaching Maddur, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the falls. The distance is around 50 kilometers, and the journey typically takes about 1-2 hours by road.

By Road:

- Shivanasamudra Falls is well-connected by road. You can reach it by taking a bus or driving from cities like Bangalore, Mysore, and Mandya. The roads leading to the falls are generally in good condition, and the journey is scenic.

- If you're driving from Bangalore, you can follow the Bangalore-Mysore Highway (NH275) to reach the falls. The distance from Bangalore to Shivanasamudra Falls is approximately 130 kilometers, and the journey typically takes around 3-4 hours.

Local transportation options are available near the falls, making it convenient to explore the area. Shivanasamudra Falls is a popular day-trip destination for visitors from nearby cities, offering a chance to escape into the tranquility of nature and witness the grandeur of these majestic falls.
