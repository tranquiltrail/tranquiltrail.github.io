Our Story

Welcome to our Website's About page. We developed this website as a part of a project exhibition for our BTech programme in 2023. Our website is a unique attempt at helping our users discover the lesser-known destinations of India that might offer them the kind of travelling experience that is unconventional and highly underrated.
We also aim at helping the tourism of the various states of India and of India in general by promoting the places that hold potential to become tourism hotspots in near future.
